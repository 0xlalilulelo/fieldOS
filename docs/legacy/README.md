- **`brief.md`** — the Field OS design brief. 610 lines covering the
  ethos (Snow Leopard refinement + Big Sur visual + MGS3 tactical
  warmth + TempleOS technical core + Apple HIG interaction). Most of
  the visual-identity and interaction-principles content carries
  forward to Arsenal verbatim and lives in ARSENAL.md § "Visual
  Identity (Preserved)" and CLAUDE.md § "Hard constraints" #4. The
  HolyC + line-count + single-language sections are obsolete. A
  fresh design brief for Arsenal will be written when Stage
  compositor work begins at M2 (~24 months out); until then, this
  document is the substantive prose articulation of the design
  philosophy and is worth reading for that context.

This document is **not** maintained going forward. Edits go in the
post-pivot canonical references.
